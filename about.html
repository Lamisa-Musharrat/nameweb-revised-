About Us

Noujontro.com is a platform aimed to bring together the necessary documents for the students of the department of Naval Architecture and Marine Engineering, BUET. Access to books, class notes and question papers of different Level-Terms can be found through this website. The website also hosts NAME 338 project files and Theses done by the students of the department.
